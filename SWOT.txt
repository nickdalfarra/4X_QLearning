STRENGTHS
- model-free
- effecient to train and execute
- independent of emotion

WEAKNESSES
- "cheat sheet" approach (discrete, finite state and action spaces) 
    forces the team to perform time-intensive feature engineering.
- trade precision decreases as wealth increases
- to improve trade precision, our agent must trade small enought amounts
    (and must be uncommon enough) so that it does not influence the market
- relies on local stationarity
- cannot perform on-line training
- cannot take advantage of market sentiments, completely impartial

OPPORTUNITIES
- make 
- implement Q-Learning for trade execution, not trade deciding
